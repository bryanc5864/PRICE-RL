\section{Experimental setup}
\label{sec:experiments}

We test three pre-registered hypotheses corresponding to the three theorems, and we extend each into a matching scaled study in §\ref{sec:large_scale} so no headline conclusion rests on a single small experiment. \textbf{H1 (low-data SOTA, partial):} PRICE-RL matches state-of-the-art active-learning baselines on $149{,}361$-variant GB1 four-mutation \citep{wu2016adaptation} under a 5-round / 100-query budget, and the adaptive-mixing ablation collapses to near-random, isolating the controller as the active mechanism. \textbf{H2 (reward-hacking diagnostic):} on a deliberately weak proxy surrogate, $\rho_t$ saturates earlier than the proxy ensemble variance signal used by $\delta$-CS \citep{kim2025deltacs}. \textbf{H3 (theory--empirics match):} on NK landscapes \citep{kauffman1989nk} swept across $K\in\{0,1,3,5,10,15,19\}$, the empirical mean $\rho_t$ tracks the analytic $\rho^*_t$ as $K$ varies.

\paragraph{Datasets.} \textbf{GB1} via FLIP \citep{dallago2021flip}; \textbf{GFP} \citep{sarkisyan2016local}, \textbf{TEM-1 $\beta$-lactamase} \citep{stiffler2015evolvability}, and \textbf{AAV} \citep{bryant2021deep} via ProteinGym \citep{notin2023proteingym}; \textbf{BCL11A enhancer} \citep{kircher2019saturation} via MaveDB \citep{esposito2019mavedb}; synthetic \textbf{NK} ($N\!\in\!\{20,40\}$, alphabet $A=4$, deterministic Blake2b-keyed contributions, $L_{\mathrm{NK}}(K) = N/(K+1)$); the deceptive \textbf{Trap-K} family for cross-domain testing.

\paragraph{Baselines and ablations.} \textbf{Random}, \textbf{AdaLead} \citep{sinai2020adalead} (greedy mutate-from-best, $\kappa = 0.05$, 8 parents), \textbf{PEX} \citep{ren2022proximal} (proximal exploration, radius~3), and a faithful \textbf{GFlowNet-AL} reimplementation \citep{jain2022biological} at the larger budgets. Ablations: \textbf{PRICE-RL ($\alpha_S=\alpha_T$)} fixes the mixing, removing only the controller; \textbf{open-loop} sets $\alpha_S \propto \rho^*_t$ without feedback; \textbf{random-support} uses a random partition with the same in-support fraction. Statistical comparisons use 10{,}000-resample paired bootstrap and Mann--Whitney U-tests; non-inferiority for AdaLead within $1$ std.
